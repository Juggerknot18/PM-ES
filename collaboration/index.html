Collaboration

Focused technical collaboration is welcome

PM-ES welcomes focused, evidence-based exchanges with Fab Labs and makerspaces; university and research laboratories; research groups; independent researchers and independent engineers; electrical engineering communities; and open hardware and open engineering communities.

Useful contribution areas

Collaboration should remain respectful of safety boundaries, public evidence and publication rights. Relevant fields include power electronics, permanent-magnet electrical machines, embedded systems.

  • Relevant literature and paper sharing
  • Public architecture review
  • Modelling and simulation methods
  • Test methodology and instrumentation
  • EMC and signal-integrity review
  • Machine modelling
  • Mechanical fabrication support
  • Reproducibility and fault-analysis feedback
  • Documentation and accessibility review
