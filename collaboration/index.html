Collaboration

Focused technical collaboration is welcome

PM-ES welcomes collaboration with Fab Labs, universities, research groups, independent engineers and open engineering communities. Collaboration should be evidence-based, respectful of safety boundaries and explicit about publication rights.

Useful contribution areas

  • Relevant literature and paper sharing
  • Public architecture review
  • Modelling and simulation methods
  • Test methodology and instrumentation
  • EMC and signal-integrity review
  • Machine modelling
  • Mechanical fabrication support
  • Reproducibility and fault-analysis feedback
  • Documentation and accessibility review
